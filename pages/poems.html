Enlightenment endarken

How do you write a symphony in four words?
"...fine waxed cotton jacket..."

Play on—'til my hand itself lend soot.


~

Volé de feu

If I turned in mine self,
it would smell of like dirt.

And the ripped hem soft sandblast
would cry out in ecclesiastical sunburst.


~

Bleecker St. Lullably

Lay me down astraddle the Genesee
street—that the Moon greet my eyes
and the Sun   _____   my feet.


~

Copper-nape doves

As does allusion to empire fall,
as does illusion on empire rest,
on a pavement patch in the shadow
of a three-tiered deceit, and the silence of dove-calls.


~

Ex

Wherefore art thou Sinner and
in lingis franca obscura do I now rest my case.
