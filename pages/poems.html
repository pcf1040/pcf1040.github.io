Enlightenment endarken

How do you write a symphony in four words?
"...fine waxed cotton jacket..."

Play on—'til my hand itself lend soot.

Volé de feu

If I turned in my guns,
it would smell of like dirt.

And the ripped hem soft sandblast
would cry out in ecclesiastical sunburst.
