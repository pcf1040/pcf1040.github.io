Bleecker St. Lullaby

A-cross the Genesee Street lay me down
that the Moon greet my eyes
and the Sun   _____   my feet.

~
Clover St. Lullaby

He walked down a Rochester street,
with New York City on his shoes
all the while a thread through it all
called Genesee Street.

All the while sick.
All the while hurting.
A sullen-yet song
ambled in reciprocal motions
called Genesee Street.

Publick Markets open wide jaws
and the bus to Rome won't save you
from a place called Genesee Street.

~
James St. Lullaby

Copper-nape doves rest in
cursory illusion, sharing time
between the Fort and the Garage
such playful birds who, uncaged,
follow careful whims
and flee from what hurt.
